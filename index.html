Barbadian Netballers

Follow The Gems as they get ready for The World Cup 2019 in Liverpool England!

The Barbados team is currently ranked 11th in the world with the only two other Caribbean teams in the top fifteen being Jamaica and Trinidad & Tobago (ranked 3rd and 10th respectively). The team is coached by veteran netballer Margaret Cutting and ably led by captain Latonia Blackman. The Netball World Cup will be held July 12th - 21st 2019.
